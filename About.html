Our Story of Taste

Welcome to the home of delicious gourmet burgers.Diner's Inn is a modern contemporary restaurant that exists to delight passionate food lovers. Walk in and place your order and sit back and relax and soak in our attractive ambiance as you wait for your order to be prepared. All the food is cooked to order and we pride ourselves on serving fresh, delicious and hygienic burgers that tantalize the taste buds and excite your senses. Our packaging is designed to be easy to hold and carry as well as ensure that your burgers maintain their flavour and shape.

Building a legacy takes it's time, effort, consistency and dedication.

With just a thought in mind to deliver with creativity and the taste Burger Lovers crave, We started with a simple take away outlet.

From introducing the american burger smash technique into the market, serving delicious mouth watering burgers with saucy and scrumptious fries, doing it all!

Our Key to success has solely been Innovating and experimenting, living up to our name ''BURGER LAB".

The Brand has made a name for itself and is settling all across Pakistan!

Not long from now, we wish to establish it in the near future as an International Burger Franchise.
